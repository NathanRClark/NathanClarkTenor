About Me

As a tenor and classical vocalist currently studying Vocal Performance at Baylor University, I am passionate about bringing classical music to life through powerful performances and meaningful connections with audiences.

View Resume

At Baylor, I am proud to be a member of the Baylor Concert Choir, where I've had the privilege of collaborating with exceptional musicians and performing in prestigious venues. Our appearances at NACDA and CBNDA, featuring large-scale symphonic works, have been particularly memorable highlights of my journey.

My dedication to music extends beyond the concert hall to St. Peter's Catholic Student Center, where I serve as both a cantor and tenor section leader in the music ministry. This role allows me to combine my love for music with my spiritual commitment.

One of my most cherished experiences was touring with the St. Paul Choral Scholars across Austria, where I had the extraordinary opportunity to perform in historic cathedrals, including the magnificent Salzburg Cathedral and St. Stephen's Cathedral in Vienna. This transformative journey deepened my appreciation for sacred music and its rich cultural heritage.

When I'm not performing or practicing, you'll find me pursuing my passion for physical fitness, whether that's at the gym, engaging in sports, or challenging myself to reach new personal bests. I believe in the strong connection between physical wellness and vocal performance.
